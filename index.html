About Spiderman

Spider-Man's secret identity is Peter Parker, a teenage high school student who lives with his Aunt May and Uncle Ben. After being bitten by a radioactive spider, Parker gains spider-like abilities, including superhuman strength, agility, and the ability to climb walls.

Spider-Man has appeared in many comic book series, including The Amazing Spider-Man, Spectacular Spider-Man, and Ultimate Spider-Man. He has also been featured in several animated television shows and films, including the recent Marvel Cinematic Universe movies.
